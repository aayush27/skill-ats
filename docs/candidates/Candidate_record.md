# Candidate details

Click a candidate from the list, board, or AI Search results to open their record. The same view opens as a card on the board or as a full page.

## What’s on the page

**Left side**

- Photo, name, and board
- Contact details (email, phone, LinkedIn, location) — use **Edit** to change them
- Dates: applied, last contact, and reminder (if set)
- Status (Open, Hired, or Rejected)
- Whether they appear on other boards
- GDPR compliance summary
- Overall rating (from quick questions, reviews, and tests)

**Top bar**

- **Renew GDPR approval**
- Stage dropdown (move them on the current board)
- Status dropdown (Open / Hired / Rejected)
- **…** menu — Copy card, Move card, Copy link, Reminder, Remove from job

**Main tabs**

Summary · Attachments · Tests · Messages · Reviews · AI Analysis (when linked to a job)

**Also always available:** Notes & Activity

## Copy card

Use **… → Copy card** when you want the same person on another board **without removing** them from the current one.

1. Open **Copy card**.
2. Confirm or edit the **Name**.
3. Choose the destination **Board** and **Stage**.
4. Under **Keep**, tick what to bring across: Attachments, Review, Notes, Messages (all on by default).
5. Click **Copy card**.

You’ll get a confirmation when it’s done, and the new copy opens.

## Move card

Use **… → Move card** to send the candidate to another board **and remove** them from the current one.

The form is the same as Copy card (name, board, stage, and what to keep). Confirm with **Move card**.

!!! tip
Prefer **Copy** if they should stay visible on both boards. Prefer **Move** if they should only live on the new board.

## Reminder

Use **… → Reminder** to set a follow-up date and notify colleagues.

1. Pick a date (past dates aren’t allowed).
2. Select one or more **colleagues** to notify.
3. Optionally add a short note.
4. Click **Set Reminder**.

The reminder date appears on the left of the candidate page. If a reminder already exists, open the same menu item and choose **Unset Reminder** to clear it. Reminder notes also show up in Notes & Activity. On the reminder day, colleagues will get an email notification.

## AI Analysis

Open the **AI Analysis** tab (available when the candidate is on a job) for a **Candidate Fit Analysis**.

| Situation                    | What you do                      |
| ---------------------------- | -------------------------------- |
| No analysis yet              | Click **Analyse**                |
| Older text-only summary      | Click **Generate full analysis** |
| Full analysis already exists | Click **Reanalyse** to refresh   |

A full analysis typically shows:

- Overall fit score (out of 100) and confidence
- Short summary paragraphs (**Show more** / **Show less**)
- **Key Requirements Match** — how well they match each requirement, with strength and sources
- It fetches the requirements from the edit job page AI Analysis fields

![CandidateAIAnalysis](../assets/CandidateAIAnalysis.png)

## Notes

Open **Notes & Activity** to leave comments for your team.

1. Type your note. Use **@** to mention a colleague.
2. Optionally tick **Private note (only visible to me)** and/or **Candidate contacted (last few days)**.
3. Click **Add Note**.

Private notes show a **P** tag; contacted notes show a **C** tag. You can **Delete** your own notes.

## Activity

In the same **Notes & Activity** tab, notes and system events appear together in one timeline (newest first), including things like stage changes and reminders. Use it as a quick history of what’s happened with this candidate.

## Other useful actions

| Action          | Where                   | What it does                                                                            |
| --------------- | ----------------------- | --------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| Change stage    | Top stage dropdown      | Moves them to another column on this board                                              |
| Hire / Reject   | Status dropdown         | Sets Open, Hired, or Rejected. Rejecting may offer to send an automatic rejection email |
| Copy link       | **… → Copy link**       | Copies a link to this candidate card                                                    |
| Remove from job | **… → Remove from job** | Removes them from this job (with confirmation)                                          |
| Send tests      | **Tests** tab           | Send assessments and open results when finished                                         |
| Attachments     | **Attachments** tab     | Upload, view, download, or delete CVs and files                                         |
| Messages        | **Messages** tab        | Send and review email-style messages                                                    |
| Reviews         | **Reviews** tab         | Add or edit skill ratings and comments                                                  |
| Summary         | **Summary** tab         | Description, education, projects, skills/tags                                           |
| Renew GDPR      | Top button              | Records that the candidate renewed privacy approval                                     |

## Profiles

You can also open a fuller **profile** for a candidate (tags, applications, and more). Your own recruiter profile is where you manage account details and [AAA](../aaa/AAA_overview.md).
