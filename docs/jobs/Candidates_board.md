# The hiring board

Each job has a **board** (pipeline) — columns for your hiring stages, such as Applied, Interview, Offer, and Hired. You move candidates between columns as they progress.

Stages are set up in **Settings** under recruitment stages. See [Company settings](../settings/Company_settings.md).

## What you can do on the board

- Drag a candidate from one stage to another
- Open a candidate to see details
- Add someone to the board
- Start tests, reject, or use other actions shown on the card

![Board](../assets/Board.png)

## From application to hire

1. A candidate applies on your [career site](../career/Public_career_site.md), or you add them manually.
2. They appear on the board in an early stage.
3. You move them through stages until you hire or reject.

!!! tip
Open a job from **Jobs**, then open its board when you want to work the pipeline day to day.
