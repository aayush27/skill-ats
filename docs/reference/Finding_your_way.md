# Finding your way around

Use this page as a quick map of SkillATS.

## Top menu

| Menu item | What it’s for |
|-----------|----------------|
| **SkillATS logo** | Back to your dashboard |
| **Jobs** | List of openings, create and edit jobs |
| **Candidates** | Everyone in your talent pool; start AI Search |
| **Career Site** | Design what applicants see |

## Other useful places

| Place | How to get there | What it’s for |
|-------|------------------|---------------|
| **Dashboard** | Logo, or after login | Overview and shortcuts |
| **Settings** | Dashboard / account menu | Company, team, stages, integrations, privacy |
| **Profile** | Dashboard / account menu | Your details and AAA |
| **Hiring board** | From a job in **Jobs** | Move candidates through stages |
| **AI Search** | **Candidates** → **AI Search** | Ask for matching people in chat |
| **Candidates due for deletion** | From **Settings** | Privacy / retention follow-up |

## For applicants

Your public career site (jobs, apply form, articles) is separate from the recruiter app. You edit it under **Career Site**; applicants never need a SkillATS recruiter login to apply.
