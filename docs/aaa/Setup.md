# Set up AAA

## Before you start

- You’re signed in as a recruiter
- You can create a forwarding rule in Outlook, Gmail, or similar
- You know how your team likes to shortlist (skills, location, seniority, ratings, etc.)

## Steps

### 1. Open your profile

From the dashboard or account menu, open **Profile** (your own profile — not another person’s).

### 2. Find AI Assignment Analyzer (AAA)

You’ll see:

- Your **forwarding address** (unique to you)
- A text box for your **prompt** — instructions used for every assignment

### 3. Copy the forwarding address

Click **Copy** and keep the address private. Only you should forward mail to it.

### 4. Create an email rule

In Outlook (or your mail app), forward broker/assignment emails to that address. For example:

- When the sender is a known broker, **or**
- When the subject mentions an assignment

→ Forward to your SkillATS AAA address.

### 5. Write and save your prompt

Describe what a good match looks like for your team. Save with **Save AAA settings**. The prompt cannot be empty.

## Checklist

- [ ] Address copied
- [ ] Forwarding rule is on and you tested with a sample email
